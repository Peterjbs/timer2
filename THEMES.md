## üé® Color Theory & Design Principles

### Saturation Levels
- **High Energy:** 1.6-1.8x saturation (Toxic, Neon, Blood)
- **Balanced:** 1.3-1.5x saturation (Cosmic, Ocean, Sunset)
- **Subtle:** 0.7-0.9x saturation (Vintage, Mono)

### Contrast Ratios
- All themes maintain WCAG AA compliance for text readability
- Accent colors provide 4.5:1 minimum contrast ratio
- Background overlays use transparency for depth

### Animation Timing
- **Fast:** 0.3-1s (Glitch, Fire, Toxic)
- **Medium:** 1.5-2.5s (Neon, Ocean, Cosmic)
- **Slow:** 3-4s (Warm, Wave)
